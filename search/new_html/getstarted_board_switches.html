Dev kit board required switch settings:

  • XMODEM - off
  • UART2 - on (this is the default for Legato serial (console) communications)
  • CAN-2 LS - on
  • POWERON - high
  • BOOT - high
  • 5V_CAN - on
  • CAN-1 HS - on
  • SW300 - on
  • ETH - on
  • SDIO/CARD - on
  • AR7/WP7 - on (on means switches are in down position; AR6/WP6 are in up position)
